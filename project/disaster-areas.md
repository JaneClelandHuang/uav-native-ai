# Disaster Scenes for UAV Simulation Projects

This document lists several real-world disasters that are suitable for
computer vision, autonomy, and mission planning projects using ESRI
imagery.

Many of these events are available through the ArcGIS Living Atlas
Disaster Imagery Explorer.

---

# 1. Los Angeles Wildfires

**Location:** Los Angeles, California, USA

**Center**
Latitude: 34.0522
Longitude: -118.2437

**Date**
January 2025

**Disaster Type**
Wildfire

**Description**

Multiple fast-moving wildfires destroyed thousands of homes across
the Pacific Palisades and Altadena areas. The imagery contains burn
scars, damaged neighborhoods, road closures, and vegetation loss.

**Suggested Computer Vision Tasks**

- Burn scar detection
- Building damage assessment
- Road accessibility
- Search-area planning
- Vegetation classification

**Interesting Mission Ideas**

- Generate safe flight corridors
- Search for isolated neighborhoods
- Plan emergency supply delivery
